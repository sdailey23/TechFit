About Us

FitTech International was founded on 1 July 2021 by computer science major Shawn Dailey with the aspiration of incoporating tech in the fitness industry.


Leaving our mark on the fitness community

As such an essential part of one's health, fitness will always be a part of who we are. However, we understand that fitness can also be an overwhelming venture. Here at FitTech we are dedicated to easing that burden and creating a better experience for fitness enthusiast at all levels. Through tech, we strive to make fitness systems better accesible in residential environments as well as provide commercial systems that are more user friendly.
